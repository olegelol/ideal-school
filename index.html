An ideal school, is it possible?

Learning is a difficult process that requires a certain approach and desire, but a good teacher can make anyone a Master of science

Hhere is no such teaching methodology in our state yet, for example, we can take the Finnish education system, which is adaptive and finds an approach to each student

but it's not just about students that we need to think about, teachers also play a huge role in the educational process, it's necessary that they don't overwork and burn out. How to achieve this? It is necessary that everyone clearly does only their work, so that teachers do not do unnecessary paperwork and do not work more than 5 hours, it is also important that the teacher will not be able to convey information of the same quality for too many students, because of this, the class should not exceed 10 people

Schedule

lessons should be at a convenient time for everyone, because everyone has a different time for better learning

lessons

the schedule should be such that it does not exhaust students and that concentration is not lost

why?

our education system does not know this word. everyone has different goals for life and it is impossible to develop a universal learning template that will be effective for everyone. "Tell me — I'll forget, show me — I'll remember, captivate me — and I'll understand" is an incredibly truthful quote, our system is not going to captivate someone but is trying to make everyone know-it-all, it doesn't work, and those who are trying to understand and learn everything become morally exhausted. really smart is not the one who knows everything, but the one who can endlessly improve himself and his mind

"from call to call" is a vicious circle, no one gets pleasure from it, and without pleasure it is impossible to understand any, even the simplest information

What's next?

from whom to take an example?

an example can be taken from the Finnish, German, English, American and French education systems, they are slightly different from each other, but all show positive results and do not exhaust the majority of students. of course, they were created with the help of huge financial opportunities of Europeans, but is money a big problem when it comes to the future generation?

In general

to summarize everything, one thing can be said, it is difficult to study, but it is feasible for everyone, you just need to find the right approach, Teachers are also people and you can't completely waste their resource. We can't forget about the students either, after all they are our future
